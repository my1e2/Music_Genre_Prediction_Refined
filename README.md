Content:

Your final report must include the following:

Provide a succinct title for your project.

Explain the motivating question you are answering, what specific stakeholder has this question, and how the answer to this question informs a specific decision, so the reader knows what your actionable insight is.

Explain what data would be ideal for answering this question, and then explain what data you were able to collect; where did it come from, how did you clean or extend it?
What methods from the course did your project build on? This section should describe the rational behind and what methods you applied and list methods from at least two modules covered in class.

Explain what analysis you did to answer your motivating question. What models did you apply, how did you evaluate them, etc.?

Explain what answer you have for the stakeholder who needs to make a decision using the answer you have provided. This explanation is often greatly aided by charts of experiments, but you should also include a clear description of your insights.

Conclude with a discussion of the limitations of your project, the data, your analysis, ethics, etc.
